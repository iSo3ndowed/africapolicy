meet the team

Daniel Ogunniyi

Dr Daniel Ogunniyi is the Founder and Managing Director of Afrika Policy Consulting. His work focuses on delivering research and strategic policy advice to governments, corporate entities and private actors on African law and policy. Daniel has a background in academia and international law practice having worked with various universities and UN agencies in nearly a dozen countries. Before founding Afrika Policy, Daniel had a flourishing career working with varied organisations including the UNICEF Office of Research in Florence, Italy, Office of the Legal Adviser, Organisation for the Prohibition of Chemical Weapons in The Hague, Netherlands, The Rights Lab at the University of Nottingham, UK, the Centre for Human Rights, University of Pretoria, South Africa, as well as Redeemer’s University in Nigeria. He holds a PhD in Law from the University of Antwerp, Belgium, and is a qualified advocate of the Supreme Court of Nigeria.
